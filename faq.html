Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is FundsIndia Academy?

    FundsIndia Academy is an investor education initiative by FundsIndia.com. The Academy offers comprehensive certified courses on personal finance, investing and financial planning. Our courses empower you with the practical knowledge that help you manage your money efficiently.

  • What is FundsIndia?

    FundsIndia is India’s first online-only investment platform. Our online investment portal provides investors a seamless and hassle-free environment that enables them to confidently invest in variety of financial instruments with ease and clarity. To know more, visit www.fundsindia.com

  • Do I need to be a FundsIndia investor to enroll in the courses?

    FundsIndia Academy is open to everyone in India, and you are free to enroll irrespective of whether or not you are a FundsIndia investor

  • How do I get access to FIA’s courses?

    Simple. Register with www.fundsindia.com/academy to kick-start your courses.

  • Do the courses come with some eligibility criteria?

    Motivation to learn - other than this, there are no specific eligibility criteria to take our courses.

  • Can I pause a course and resume it whenever I am free?

    Of course you can. All our courses are available completely on demand. You can complete them at your convenience.

  • What do I do if I forget my password?

    Click on “Forgot password” and specify your email ID. We will email you a reset link with which you can reset your password.

  • Do I get a certificate on completion of my course?

    Yes, you will be awarded a certificate on successful completion of your course.

  • Do my certificates come with a validity period?

    No. All our certificates are valid forever.

  • How do I earn a course certificate?

    Login to www.fundsindia.com/academy. Pick a course of your choice, complete it and earn your certificate online.

  • Do I have to pay for these courses?

    No. All our courses are available for free.

  • What is FACE?

    FundsIndia Academy Corporate Education (FACE) is a corporate outreach initiative of FundsIndia Academy. In our FACE programme, we conduct workshops in organizations, educating the corporates on how to identify and select the right investment plans to suit their needs.

  • How do I organize a FACE in my organization?

    Give us a call at 7667 166 166, or write to us at face@fundsindia.com. We’ll bring FACE to your organization.

  • I am not a working professional. Am I still eligible to take these courses?

    Totally. Anyone with an interest to learn more about financial planning and investing can enrol in our courses.
